Four key techniques (cornerstones) to CT:
decomposition - breaking down a complex problem or system into smaller, more manageable parts
 pattern reconition - looking for similarities among and within problems
abstraction-focusing on the important information only.ignoring irrelevant detail algorithms 
developing a step-by- step solution to the problem, or the rules to follow to solve the problem
 Examples of things we usually do every day
Cooking Fried Noodle 
Decomposition: in the initial stage of noodles, we usually prepare steps to solve the problem by preparing ½ liter in the pan 
Pattern recognition: pattern recognition by understanding the process of cooking noodles from boiling the water first, opening the noodle wrapper, opening the seasoning packet until the noodles are drained and put on the plate. 
Abstract: a view of how much water is needed to cook noodles and how long it takes for the noodles to cook 
Algorithm: already understand the pattern from above, add enough water, heat the water, open the package of noodles and seasonings, then add the noodles and wait a few moments until the noodles are cooked
